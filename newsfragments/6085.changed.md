* PyO3 threads now hang instead of `pthread_exit` trying to acquire the GIL when the interpreter is shutting down after detaching. This mimics the [Python 3.14](https://github.com/python/cpython/issues/87135) behavior and avoids undefined behavior and crashes and applies the same logic as done for [Python::attach](https://github.com/PyO3/pyo3/pull/4874).
